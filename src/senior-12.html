Being a student in the Burnett Honors College has its benefits, including the opportunity to live alongside other likeminded honors students. First-year Burnett Honors Scholars have the option of choosing the Honors Living Learning Community at The Towers at Knights Plaza.
Why Choose Honors Housing?
  • Live and learn alongside other honors students.
  • Network with faculty and staff from the Burnett Honors College and enjoy access to an exclusive residential computer lab for Burnett Honors Scholars.
  • Take advantage of classes and enrichment programs held in the comfort of your residence hall.
The Towers Lifestyle
Enjoy an apartment-style residence with full kitchen, single bedrooms, frequent housekeeping and 24-hour security. The Towers at Knights Plaza is nestled in one of the most dynamic areas of campus.
